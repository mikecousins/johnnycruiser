This game was developed to be fairly simple, but include Seafarers of Catan. It consists of two continents separated by an ocean that is 2 hexes wide. Each continent includes a (complete) variety of resource hexes. The production number tokens are split evenly between the continents, but then placed randomly on the resource hexes.

To encourage players to cross the ocean, the first player to connect the two continents receives 2 VP, while the remaining players can earn 1 VP when they first connect the continents. 14 VP wins the game It isn't critical to cross the ocean, as often the winner developes on one continent only and wins through cities, longest road and/or harbor master.
