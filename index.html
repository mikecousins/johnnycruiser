Our family through the years

The purpose of this site is to document our travels, interests and hobbies, and to share this with our friends. If you have any comments or suggestions (or just to keep in touch), please email us.

Now that I (John) have retired, and Marj is nearing retirement, travel (especially cruising) has become a bigger and bigger part of our lives. Hence, most of the following pages are related in some way to travel. While we are both "young" (at heart) and healthy, we have a yearning to travel the globe.

Just click on the links at the top or left side of the page to enter a part of our world. Some of the pages may be somewhat dated, but we work on them as we have time (and interest). Mike has his own Web site called Mike Cousins Photography.
